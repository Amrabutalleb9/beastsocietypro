1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognise your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and settings.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The Site cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with the Site. These cookies help us improve the Site by collecting information about pages visited, time spent on pages, and any error messages encountered.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings (such as cookie consent preference) to provide a more personalised experience.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be used to track your browsing habits and deliver advertising relevant to your interests. We may use third-party services that set their own cookies for this purpose.
